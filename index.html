About Us

Caravana Game Studios is an indie video game development startup based in Argentina. We are dedicated to creating unique gaming experiences, combining captivating narratives with innovative mechanics. We believe in the power of games to inspire, connect, and transport players to new and unforgettable worlds. Our team strives to push the boundaries of creativity and technology to deliver high-quality titles that will resonate with a global audience.
